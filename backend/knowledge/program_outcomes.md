# Program Outcomes and Effectiveness

Approved outcome data by program area, for use in substantiating impact claims in appeals.

## Foster network

The foster network grew from 210 to 340 active homes in 2025. Fostered animals are adopted 40% faster than those housed only in the shelter, and foster placement reduced per-animal care costs by an average of $180 over a typical stay.

## Spay and neuter

Every $65 funds one spay or neuter procedure. Independent modeling estimates each procedure prevents an average of six future unwanted animals over three years, making prevention the highest-leverage use of donor funds.

## Community education

School and community programs reached 22,000 people in 2025 with responsible-pet-ownership education. Communities that hosted a program saw a 12% year-over-year drop in owner surrenders.

## Retention of impact

Recurring monthly donors fund roughly 60% of core operating costs, providing the predictable revenue that lets us commit to animals with long or expensive courses of treatment.

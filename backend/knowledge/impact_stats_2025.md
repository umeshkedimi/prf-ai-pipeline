# 2025 Impact Report — Key Statistics

Last year our community of donors made a measurable difference. These figures are drawn from our audited annual report and are cleared for use in donor communications.

## Animals rescued and rehomed

In 2025 we took in 4,812 animals across our shelter network and found permanent homes for 4,190 of them — a 92% placement rate, our highest ever. The average length of stay dropped from 34 days to 21 days thanks to expanded foster capacity.

## Cost of care

It costs an average of $42 to provide a rescued animal with one week of food, shelter, and basic veterinary care. A gift of $150 covers a full month of care for one animal, including vaccinations and spay/neuter surgery.

## Reach of the mobile clinic

Our mobile veterinary clinic delivered 11,300 low-cost or free services in underserved neighborhoods in 2025, including 3,100 spay/neuter procedures that prevented an estimated 18,000 future unwanted litters.

## Where the money goes

87 cents of every dollar donated goes directly to programs; 9 cents to fundraising and 4 cents to administration. We have held program spending above 85% for six consecutive years.

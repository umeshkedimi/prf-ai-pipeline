# Donor-Funded Success Stories

These stories are approved for use in appeals. They illustrate the concrete outcomes donor gifts make possible.

## Bailey's second chance

Bailey, a senior labrador surrendered when her owner entered hospice, arrived with an untreated hip condition. Donor gifts funded the surgery and three months of rehabilitation. She was adopted by a retired couple and now serves as a certified therapy dog visiting the same hospice that once cared for her first family.

## The winter shelter expansion

When a cold snap overwhelmed capacity in January, an emergency appeal raised $61,000 in nine days. Those funds let us open a temporary 40-kennel annex and take in every animal turned away by nearby full shelters. Not a single animal was left outside during the coldest week of the year.

## Mango the community cat

A feral kitten with a severe eye infection, Mango was trapped, treated, and socialized through our foster program over four months. The total cost of care was $680, funded entirely by recurring monthly donors. Mango now lives with a family who first met him at an adoption event.
